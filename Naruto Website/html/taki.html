Waterfall Country: Hidden Village in the Waterfall (Takigakure)

Waterfall Country is a small country adjacent to the the Land of Fire and Earth and Grass. The country has a small shinobi village which is hidden behind a waterfall. The village surrounds a lagoon in the center which stands the Great Tree. The Waterfall village was first featured in the Naruto anime OAV #2 (Jump Festa 2004); its shinobis are talented in water manipulation jutsus. The Akatsuki member known as Hidan is from the Waterfall village.
